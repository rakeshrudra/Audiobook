Introducing Islamic Audio Books

Islamic Audio Books is a Non-profitable Islamic publisher using different platforms to present a vivid collection of the word of Allah (سُبْحَانَهُ وَ تَعَالَى) with guidance from Authentic Hadiths of our beloved Prophet Muhammad (صلى الله عليه وسلم), Quotes of Sahaba (رضي الله عنه) Taba'een (رحمة الله عليه) to the modern-day busy Muslim.

A three years of journey and a long-distance covered Islamic Audio Books started in 2017 -Audio recording of the book "Tafseer Ibn E Katheer" from Arabic to Urdu by Maulana Muhammad Junagarhi, followed by Shamail e Muhammadiyya and Daily Azkar & Du'as. Alhamdulillah we have completed our latest project Mishkat Al Masabih as well. إِنْ شَاءَ ٱللَّٰهُ more books in different languages to follow.
